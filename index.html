About Me

Hello, I'm Francis De Leon, a motivated individual from Pangasinan, Philippines. I hold a Senior High diploma from PSU (Pangasinan State University) and am currently pursuing my college education at PHINMA UPANG Dagupan. My fascination with video games sparked my interest in computers, and I've since honed my technical skills. Beyond the screen, I find joy in exploring cinematic narratives, maintaining an active lifestyle through gym sessions, and starting each day with a brisk morning walk. Rooted in Stoicism, I believe in seeking contentment and tranquility in life. My peers often describe me as creatively unconventional, with a tendency to engage in contemplation. Embracing the words, "To win TRUE freedom one must be a slave to philosophy," I value profound thinking as a pathway to personal growth. I am dedicated to achieving a state of harmony and fulfillment within myself and in my interactions with the world around me.

First-Aid

First AID stands as a revolutionary application designed to empower individuals with the ability to potentially save lives, even in the face of unfamiliar emergency situations. This app serves as a virtual lifeline, offering step-by-step guidance for those who might find themselves unprepared in critical moments. By providing clear and concise instructions, First AID equips users to take effective action, enhancing the chances of survival for those in distress. Furthermore, the app functions as a beacon of hope, not only for the injured but for the entire community. Notifying medical professionals and facilities, First AID harnesses the power of connectivity to swiftly alert individuals with the necessary expertise. In essence, First AID transcends the boundaries of medical knowledge, transforming anyone into a potential lifesaver while fostering a network of support that can make a life-saving difference..
